Context

The goal of this project is to optimise the use of the solar plant powering Site A and reduce the reliance on power bought from grid. The site should buy from the grid as little as possible, and preferably only when the rate is low (during overnight hours). This will be done by forecasting the solar output of the plant and the site load using deep learning models. The predictions can then be used to optimise the discharge of the battery, and work out when power needs to be bought.

Business Case

Analysis of cost savings from optimization will provide business case.

Data Sources

For predicting the solar output and site load, data will be sourced from weather APIs.

In addition, data from the site's data collection system will also be used as further features for models
